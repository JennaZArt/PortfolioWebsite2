Jenna Zielinski
is a Graphic Designer from Whitby, Ontario. She graduated summa cum laude from McMaster University with a Bachelor of Arts in Multimedia and Cognitive Science of Language.

During her final year of university, Jenna worked as a Teaching Assistant for the course Making Art and Understanding Technology & Images and volunteered as a Graphic Designer for the Communications & Media Arts Society. Following graduation, she participated in the Humanities Career Apprenticeship Program. This involved a year-long engagement at Hamilton Health Sciences Foundation where Jenna held the role of Marketing Coordinator. In that role, she was responsible for a broad range of creative tasks including the design of social media graphics, digital ads, posters, and more. She also wrote copy and assisted with website design and maintenance.
