Jenna Zielinski
is a Graphic Designer and Illustrator from Whitby, Ontario. She graduated summa cum laude from McMaster University with a Bachelor of Arts in Multimedia and Cognitive Science of Language.

In her final year of university, Jenna worked as a Teaching Assistant for the course Making Art and Understanding Technology & Images. Following graduation, she participated in the Humanities Career Apprenticeship Program. This involved a year-long engagement at Hamilton Health Sciences Foundation where Jenna held the role of Marketing Coordinator. In that role, she was responsible for a broad range of creative tasks including the design of social media graphics, digital ads, and posters. She also wrote copy and assisted with website design and maintenance.
