Campaign Overview

Organization: McMaster Children's Hospital Foundation
Event: MacKids Walk & Wheel
Event Logo by: Derek Fryer
Photography by: Carole & Roy Timm
Software: Adobe Illustrator

About

MacKids Walk & Wheel is an annual fundraising event run in support of McMaster Children's Hospital Foundation. The event is jam-packed with family fun and includes bike rides for children, an all-ages walk, bouncy castles, face painting, and more. I designed several marketing materials for the event including two digital billboards, a transit shelter ad, two posters, and a tent card. These were displayed inside McMaster Children's Hospital and around the city of Hamilton.

The Goal

The objective of the campaign was to raise awareness for the event and encourage registrations and fundraising. The target audience included patients of McMaster Children's Hospital and their families, as well as other Hamilton residents interested in supporting the hospital. The designs should be visually consistent, represent the fun atmosphere of the event, and deliver the information in a clear and logical manner.

Design

The information on the designs was organized in the following order:

  • What: Walk & Wheel
  • When: Saturday, June 3, 2023
  • Where: Bayfront Park
  • Why: To make a meaningful impact for patients and their families.
  • How: Scan the QR code or visit the website.
The layout was carefully adjusted to suit the dimensions of each piece and ensure a visually balanced result while retaining the hierarchy of information. Bright colour blocking was used for the background to catch the eye of children and families and to communicate the vibrant and exciting feeling of the event.
