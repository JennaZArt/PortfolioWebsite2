About

The Communications & Media Arts Society is a club at McMaster University that provides an opportunity for students studying or interested in the fields of Communications and/or Media Arts to network, connect with other students with similar interests, and develop the professional skills required to succeed in their ideal careers.

Designs

As the society's events and content are a combination of fun and professional, I wanted to evoke that feeling in these designs. To achieve this, I included lively patterns, but kept the compositions and shapes simple.

I used a timeline layout for the infographics because the information was in chronological order, making it ideal for this format. The text and icons switch from left to right for each section, which creates movement, making the design more dynamic.
