Project Overview

Organization: Hamilton Health Sciences Foundation
Creative concept by: 20Eight Digital Marketing Agency
Software: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro

About

The 50/50 Inspiration Lottery allows individuals to purchase tickets for the chance to win a cash prize while supporting the hospitals associated with Hamilton Health Sciences. To advertise the November 2022 Inspiration Lottery, I created two animated ads. These ads were featured on digital screens in restobars and residential buildings across Hamilton. In addition to these animated ads, I was also responsible for the design of five flights of display ads and Meta ads and headers for emails. This contributed to the November 2022 Inspiration Lottery overachieving the revenue goal by 23%, raising $104,750 total.

The Goal

Research has shown that audiences need to view an ad approximately 7 times before they take action to buy. The goal of the digital screen ads was to provide another avenue to reach potential buyers and increase their number of impressions, ultimately hoping to convert impressions to ticket purchases.

Design

20Eight's data from previous lotteries concluded that people respond best to ads with human faces and money. These were the two key images included. The animations were kept minimal and slow-paced to avoid them becoming overwhelming and distracting. The most important text components are static and never leave the screen (the tagline and the call to action), while the less crucial information slides in and changes every few seconds.
