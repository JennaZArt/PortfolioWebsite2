About

Strides is an annual fundraising event run in support of Hamilton General Hospital, including the Regional Rehabilitation Centre, and St. Peter's Hospital. Individuals and teams fundraise prior to the event and take part in a leisurely walk or a timed run on the event day. The social media toolkit included six square posts for use across social platforms and six stories for Instagram. Also included were captions that individuals could copy and paste onto their posts.

The Goal

This social media toolkit was created to provide a quick and easy way for participants to encourage donations to their fundraisers and to raise awareness for the event. The priorities when designing the toolkit were to make the graphics legible, accessible, and applicable to as many participants as possible.

Design

Bold, uppercase text and snappy lines aimed to quickly capture the attention of those scrolling on social media. Font sizes were kept fairly large to assure readability on small mobile screens. The Instagram stories also provided white space for participants to add their own text and the link to their fundraisers. While design elements were kept to a minimum, the "swoop" motif along the bottom third of the images had the dual purpose of adding visual interest and providing space for the logo and hashtag that balanced the text on the graphics.
